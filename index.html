Red Pasta Specialty

This is a wonderfully spicy pasta overflowing with creamy tomato-ness and a boost of protein with your shredded chicken

Ingredients

  • 200 grams cherry tomatoes
  • 1 white onion diced
  • 1/4 stick of butter
  • 2 handfuls of shredded chicken
  • chilli powder
  • italian herbs
  • 350 grams penne pasta
  • bunch of parsley, chopped
  • 200 ml thickened cream

Directions

  1. Dice the onion and half the tomatoes
  2. In a pan add the butter, onions, and tomatoes and let them cook on low heat for 10mins
  3. Meanwhile, cook the pasta
  4. Press down on the tomatoes to soften them, then add the cream
  5. Add chilli and herbs
  6. Add shredded chicken
  7. Add the pasta and a little bit of the water to aid mixing
  8. Finally top up with the parsley
